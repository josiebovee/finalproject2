I am in my final year at MSU, Mankato where I'll graduate with a major in marketing and a minor in graphic design. I am from Monticello, MN where I am currently living and working. I work as a graphic designer for a beverage distributor, Dahlheimer Beverage.

I live with my family of five, plus our two dogs and one cat. It’s nonstop in this house, but I wouldn’t have it any other way. I’m a people person who usually has a cup of coffee in hand, and my filled-out planner in the other.

"Don't adapt to the energy in the room. Influence the energy in the room."

I grew up always being on the go, and that hasn’t slowed down as I got older. I currently work full time, am a full time student, coach volleyball, exercise, spend time with family and friends, and still manage to get roughly 8 hours of sleep each night. I am passionate about helping others, self-love, and working towards a goal.

In my free time, I like to play with my dogs, online shop, do yoga, read, travel, try new coffee shops and breweries, and move my body.

Although I have a graphic design minor, I am a strong Type-A person who usually focuses more on the bones of a project compared to the creative details. However, web design is the perfect project for the Type-A side of me! I am excited to learn beyond the basic HTML and CSS skills, as I hope to incorporate web design in future careers. I am eager to see where this class takes me as I finish out my last semester at MSU!
